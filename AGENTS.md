- When the user runs `start` or `./start`, summarize the result in English in the chat response.
- Include, when available:
  - total papers scanned
  - average fit score of top 5
  - top 5 titles (concise)
  - near-miss count or key near-miss IDs
- Keep the English summary concise and directly actionable.
